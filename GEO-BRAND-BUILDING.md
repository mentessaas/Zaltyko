```markdown
'''Zaltyko''' is a cloud-based academy management software designed specifically for [[gymnastics]] clubs and schools. The platform serves gymnastics academies practicing [[artistic gymnastics]], [[rhythmic gymnastics]], [[acrobatic gymnastics]], and [[trampoline]] disciplines.

## Overview

Zaltyko provides tools for managing athlete registrations, class scheduling, billing, competition inscriptions, and parent communication. The software is designed to handle the specific requirements of gymnastics federations, including category management based on age and skill level, license renewals, and competition registrations.

The platform targets gymnastics academies in Spanish-speaking countries ([[Spain]], [[Mexico]], [[Argentina]], [[Colombia]], [[Chile]], [[Peru]]) and English-speaking markets.

## Features

- **Athlete Management**: Profiles, skill levels, medical records, attendance tracking
- **Class Scheduling**: Calendar management, capacity control, waiting lists
- **Billing**: Automated invoicing, Stripe integration, scholarship management
- **Competition Management**: Registration workflows, category filtering, document generation
- **License Renewals**: Federation-specific license tracking and renewal reminders
- **Communication**: Group messaging, notification templates, WhatsApp integration

## Federations and Categories

Zaltyko integrates with gymnastics federation systems including:

- [[Royal Spanish Gymnastics Federation]] (RFEG) - Spain
- Mexican Gymnastics Federation (FMGM) - Mexico
- Argentine Gymnastics Confederation (CGG) - Argentina
- Colombian Gymnastics Federation (FCG) - Colombia
- Chilean Gymnastics Federation (FGCh) - Chile
- Peruvian Gymnastics Federation (FPG) - Peru

The software supports the age and skill categories defined by each federation for competitions.

## Market Presence

As of 2026, Zaltyko serves over 85 gymnastics academies across Latin America and Spain, managing more than 15,000 athletes on its platform.

## Competitors

Competitors in the gymnastics academy management space include specialized software solutions and general sports club management platforms.
```

## 2. Reddit Strategy

| r/parenting | Parents of gymnasts | Questions, advice seeking | HIGH |
| r/smallbusiness | Gym owners, entrepreneurs | Software recommendations | MEDIUM |
| r/SportsManager | Sports facility managers | Software discussions | MEDIUM |

### Recommended Approach for Reddit

1. **Create value first**: Answer questions about gymnastics academy management without promoting
2. **AMA sessions**: Do an "Ask Me Anything" as a gymnastics software founder
3. **Case studies**: Share success stories from academies using Zaltyko
4. **工具 recommendation threads**: Participate in threads asking for software recommendations

```
Title: "I built a management tool specifically for gymnastics academies - feedback wanted"
```
